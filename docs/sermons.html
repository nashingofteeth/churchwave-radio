2 EVILA

"For this is what the Lord God says: 'When I make you a desolate city, like the cities which are not inhabited, when I bring up the deep over you and the great waters cover you, then I will bring you down with those who go down to the pit, to the people of old, and I will make you remain in the lower parts of the earth, like the ancient ruins, with those who go down to the pit, so that you will not be inhabited; but I will put glory in the land of the living. I will cause you sudden terrors and you will no longer exist; though you will be sought, you will never be found again,' declares the Lord God." [Ezekiel 26:19-21]

This has been a difficult year for this country, and it is still not over. The headlines show that we are closer to the Day of the Lord than ever before, something no election cycle or military action can hope to prevent. America: it is time to get right with the Lord God. His Day is almost upon us; it cannot be stopped.

2 EVILA is the newest Message from Brother Evila Seh, set to music by plunderphonics musician WAV Rewinder.

1 EVILA

"And the Word became flesh, and dwelt among us; and we saw His glory, glory as of the only Son from the Father, full of grace and truth." [John 1:14]

"Then I looked, and I heard an eagle flying in midheaven, saying with a loud voice, 'Woe, woe, woe to those who live on the earth, because of the remaining blasts of the trumpet of the three angels who are about to sound!'" [Rev. 8:13]

Though they had collaborated before, 1 EVILA marks the first time Br. Evila took a lead role in the conception and production of a project between him and WAV Rewinder, resulting in this first full-length radio sermon from Shaking Time Ministries. A nontraditional and strangely autobiographical look at Br. Evila's foundational doctrines, this abstract program covers a wide range of topics from the nature of God to the commodification of the church.

heaven's gates

"And he carried me away in the Spirit to a great and high mountain, and showed me the holy city, Jerusalem, coming down out of heaven from God, having the glory of God. Her brilliance was like a very valuable stone, like a stone of crystal-clear jasper.It had a great and high wall, with twelve gates, and at the gates twelve angels; and names were written on the gates, which are the names of the twelve tribes of the sons of Israel. There were three gates on the east, three gates on the north, three gates on the south, and three gates on the west."
[Rev. 21:10-13]

The newest record from friend of the ministry WAV Rewinder is a relaxing ambient work where each track is named for one of the 12 precious stones that make up the foundation of the New Jerusalem. An excellent album to put on during quiet prayer or sacred meditation.

E V E N S O N G

"In peace I will both lie down and sleep,
For You alone, Lord, have me dwell in safety."
[Ps. 4:8]

This solo project by this ministry's frequent collaborator WAV Rewinder seeks to create a feeling of divine peace within the listener to remind them of the promise that, in the words of Paul, the Lord of peace may Himself give you peace in all times in every way. A perfect program for a personal Friday night vespers or Saturday sundown meditation.

WILL YOU STAND AGAINST THE WORKERS OF INIQUITY?

"So take care how you listen; for whoever has, to him more will be given; and whoever does not have, even what he thinks he has will be taken away from him." [Luke 8:18]

The first ambient record by WAV Rewinder to venture into the realm of country music, a genre dear to the heart of the musician. An excellent program to play during prayer or devotional time, or even to fall asleep. God frequently speaks to His followers through dreams, and Rewinder is able to capture a bit of that hypnagogic Spirit here for you to experience again and again.

To Receive Prayer, Press One

"I wait for the Lord, my soul waits,
And I wait for His word.
My soul waits in hope for the Lord
More than the watchmen for the morning;
Yes, more than the watchmen for the morning."
[Ps. 130:5-6]

What if Heaven put you on hold? Sometimes the Christian walk can be a frustrating one; God works on His time, not ours. For this double-length program, WAV Rewinder seeks to capture the listless feeling of waiting for answers and show how we can find joy in that lingering process itself. How long will you wait?
