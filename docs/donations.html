About Donations to Shaking Time

If you have been helped or inspired by this ministry, we give thanks that we have had an opportunity to be a blessing for you. Please know that we will never ask for direct donations to support this ministry. God has supported our Message for 6 years without the need for us to solicit you for monetary means to keep Shaking Time afloat. We do not expect to turn a profit. While we are not wealthy ourselves, except spiritually, God, whom we serve, has created all things, including currency, and He continually prepares a path forward that we might follow His leading in this time.

This is the safeguard to keep us in His will. Some would have us go this way or that, but when God does not open up the means to pursue those paths, we know it is not His will for us. We do not covet your money in order to have expensive homes, cars, planes, or yachts. We have none of those things and believe God's true servants do not need them to spread the Truth. Our only request is for your ongoing prayer support that we continue to minister according to God's will, and that our Message of dire warning to the people of Earth today will be spread to everyone who will hear before it's too late.

If you still feel God calling you to give financially, then please consider giving directly to the community through the following charities:
