Further Listening

Pantechnocracy and the Inevitable Apocalypse
by Vacation Bible School

"Woe to you who are longing for the day of the Lord,
For what purpose will the day of the Lord be to you?
It will be darkness and not light;
As when a man flees from a lion
And a bear confronts him,
Or he goes home, leans with his hand against the wall,
And a snake bites him.
Will the day of the Lord not be darkness instead of light,
Even gloom with no brightness in it?"
[Amos 5:18-20]

Foundational pioneer of the vaporwave subgenre "churchwave," Vacation Bible School's sermon-program on the Day of the Lord is a phenomenal text about the Shaking Time period we find ourselves in. A condensed look at many of the simultaneous factors forcing this planet into the end of days — technological innovation, corporate greed, and careless man-made changes to the environment, this program will keep you thinking and dancing in equal measure.

Easter Vigil
by D S C P L

"Grace, mercy, and peace will be with us, from God the Father and from Jesus Christ, the Son of the Father, in truth and love." [2 John 1:3]

D S C P L's beautiful ambient program is specifically for Easter weekend, but worthy of any sacred evening. A short, sweet, and beautiful reminder of the plan of salvation and what this life is all about.

E a r l y  S u n d a y  M o r n i n g
by HeReigns™

"Train up a child in the way he should go,
Even when he grows older he will not abandon it."
[Prov. 22:6]

Though the acrid comforts of nostalgia are one of the major forces effacing our society today, it is often a powerful tool to pull wandering believers back to the fold. But are there limits to this pull? This is one of the questions this program by HeReigns™ seeks to answer. A warm but not always reassuring listen.

Romans 13
by Vacation Bible School

"The night is almost gone, and the day is near. Therefore let's rid ourselves of the deeds of darkness and put on the armor of light." [Romans 13:12]

In this provocative first album under the "churchwave" moniker, Vacation Bible School weaves contemporary Evangelical Protestant sonic textures with direct quotes of the horrific deeds done in its name. This often nauseating and disturbing program seeks to reveal the foundational rot beneath the modern Laodicean church, that will end just as the Bible predicts.

HEAVEN CRY
by DATAGIRL

"...bring the fattened calf, slaughter it, and let's eat and celebrate; for this son of mine was dead and has come to life again; he was lost and has been found." [Luke 15:23-24]

We know the Bible says to make a joyful noise when praising God, and sometimes we can forget that, focusing instead on the grave nature of our messages about the future from His Word. It is important to remember that our music can be jubilant and triumphant. DATAGIRL's 2019 smash hit trap program is an exuberant noise indeed!

My Father's House
by upallnight

"Do not call to mind the former things,
Or consider things of the past."
[Isa. 43:18]

"Home" can be a difficult concept for many people. But when God calls us home, it will be unlike any other. Jesus said: "In My Father's house are many rooms; if that were not so, I would have told you, because I am going there to prepare a place for you." [John 14:2]

First Fruits
by Donut Sunday

"What is the outcome then? I will pray with the spirit, but I will pray with the mind also; I will sing with the spirit, but I will sing with the mind also." [1 Cor. 14:15]

David may have encouraged us to sing a new song, but sometimes a familiar classic is exactly what a believer needs. This epic triple-program contains dozens of fan-favorites from many eras, updated for a new generation. We think Donut Sunday is one to watch!
